Our Philosophy

Our Education Model

Jagat & Sawah Gyaan

Who am I ‘being’ today?

Children learn about the world and their community and connect all of that information to their life today through analysis and critical-thought. Children learn to root themselves in their own being through social, emotional learning, yoga and meditation.

Kojh & Mauz

How am I relating with the other?

Children explore life through a scientific approach and lens by questioning, hypothesizing, and experimenting. Children learn to appreciate the beauty and aesthetics of the mysteries of the world through pure observation,

Yogdaan & Abhivayqt

What role am I playing in life?

Children solve community problems by launching student-led projects and, implementing indigenous innovations. Children add beauty to the world through their artistic expressions in the domain of both visual and performing arts.
