In the 1930s, Anari Devi walked 300 km through the mountains of Uttarakhand, carrying provision for this 15 days of travel, solely to give her 5th-grade examination. I grew up hearing this story many times because it's the story of my grandmother. What breaks my heart is that even after a hundred year, the socio-economic conditions of the mountains haven't improved much.
