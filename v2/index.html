Hundreds of villages in the Doon Valley are on the verge of becoming ghost villages. 15% of people who migrate to cities do so due to the lack of schools. And, most of the those who migrate end up in urban slums.

Org Promise & Mission

Providing a balanced and contextual education to the hill children to lead lives with their Head, Heart and Hands in harmony.

We are reimagining the traditional model of schooling, with these 3 shifts:

From Compliance to Critical Thinking.
From Competition to Compassion.
From Consumption to Creation.
